Managing Domain

Change Ownership#

Navigator to the domain manage page, open the "Manage" tab, click the address to open the input box, Input a new owner address, and click "Sure" button. And confirm the wallet to sign the transaction.

Change Resolver#

Navigator to the domain manage page, open the "Resolver" tab, click the hint text to open the input box, Input a new resolver address, and click "Sure" button. And confirm the wallet to sign the transaction.

Add Cryptocurrency Address#

Input right cryptocurrency address to the right currency, and click "Save Change" button. And confirm the wallet to sign the transaction.

Set IPFS CID#

Navigator to the domain manage page, open the "Website" tab, click the hint text to open the input box, Input a new IPFS CID, and click "Sure" button. And confirm the wallet to sign the transaction.

Upload Website to IPFS#

  1. Select the website directory which you want to upload to the IPFS network.

  1. Click the "Confirm upload" button to confirm the upload process.

  1. When you upload the website success, you will got your website IPFS CID, then you can save the IPFS CID to your domains.
