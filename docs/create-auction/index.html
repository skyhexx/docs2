Create Auction

The minimum domain auction period is 24 hours.

  1. You can open the "Auction Market", and click the "Create Auction" button to create a domain auction to sell your domain.

  1. Fill in the field with your domain, the price and the auction period. Then click the "Approve" button to authorize the auction contract to spend your domain tokens..

  1. When you finished the approve operation, then you can click the "Create" button to finish the auction creating operation.
