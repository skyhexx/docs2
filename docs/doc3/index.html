Introduction#

Trust Domains token(TDT) is the ecosystem token which minted by liquidity mining. Trust Domains will buy back and burn the tokens quarterly. Also can receive the dividends of the auction platform. The total supply of the token is 20,000,000 TDT. We will burn the token to the supply 10,000,000 TDT.

Trust Domains is deployed in the Ethereum mainnet, Conflux, Binance Smart Chain and Huobi HecoChain at this stage, We will porting to the Polkadot too, this will attracting more users to experience the domains service.

We aim to building a cross chain Web3.0 financial infrastructure. So the TDT will be the gateway to decentralised DeFi world!
